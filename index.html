Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does the AI know how to answer questions about our specific treatments? +

We train the AI on your exact pricing, treatment protocols, and clinic policies. It only speaks on what it's authorized to know. If it encounters a complex medical query it cannot resolve, it automatically flags the thread for human review.

Will it sound like a generic robot? +

No. We customize the tone, style, and vocabulary to match your clinic's brand voice—whether that's professional clinical, or warm and welcoming. We also integrate patient communication templates to keep dialogues natural.

Can it integrate with our existing booking software? +

Yes, our system integrates directly with all major med spa booking calendars (Boulevard, PatientNow, Jane, Zenoti) and Google Calendars to pull open spots and schedule consultations in real-time without double-booking.

What happens if a client asks a highly complex medical question? +

The AI handles pre-booking questions. If a query requires human medical expertise, it instantly flags the conversation and routes it to your clinical team via email or SMS notification so a provider can hop on.
